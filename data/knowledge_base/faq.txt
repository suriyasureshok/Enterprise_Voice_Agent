VOXOPS LOGISTICS — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do I track my shipment?
A: Ask the voice agent "Where is my order?" followed by your order ID, or visit the web portal.

Q: What does "in_transit" status mean?
A: Your package has left the warehouse and is currently on route to the destination.

Q: What does "delayed" status mean?
A: Your package has encountered a delay due to traffic, weather, or processing issues. A revised ETA will be provided.

Q: Can I change the delivery address after the order is placed?
A: Address changes are possible before dispatch. Contact customer support immediately.

Q: How long does an investigation for a missing package take?
A: Investigations are opened within 24 hours and typically conclude within 5 business days.

Q: Can the AI agent create a support ticket?
A: Yes. Say "I need to speak to an agent" or "I want to file a complaint" to trigger an escalation.

Q: What vehicles do you use for delivery?
A: We operate a mixed fleet of vans, trucks, and motorcycles depending on package weight and route.

Q: How is the estimated delivery time calculated?
A: We use a logistics simulation engine that accounts for distance, vehicle speed, warehouse load, and real-time traffic conditions.
