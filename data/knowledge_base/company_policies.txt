VOXOPS LOGISTICS — COMPANY POLICIES

1. DELIVERY TIMEFRAMES
   - Standard delivery: 3–5 business days
   - Express delivery: 1–2 business days
   - Same-day delivery: Available in select metro areas

2. SHIPMENT TRACKING
   - All orders are assigned a tracking ID at dispatch.
   - Customers can query shipment status via voice or web portal.
   - Real-time updates are provided every 4 hours.

3. MISSING PACKAGES
   - Report missing packages within 30 days of expected delivery.
   - An investigation is launched within 24 hours of a report.
   - Replacement or refund is issued within 5–7 business days.

4. DAMAGED GOODS
   - Document damage with photos within 48 hours of delivery.
   - File a claim through the customer portal or voice agent.
   - Compensation is processed within 7–10 business days.

5. REROUTING
   - Rerouting requests must be made before dispatch.
   - Post-dispatch rerouting may incur a fee up to $25.

6. ESCALATION
   - Complex issues are escalated to a human agent automatically.
   - Agent response time is guaranteed within 2 business hours.
