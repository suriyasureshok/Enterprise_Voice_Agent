VOXOPS LOGISTICS — COMPANY POLICIES

1. DELIVERY TIMEFRAMES
   - Standard delivery: 3–5 business days
   - Express delivery: 1–2 business days
   - Same-day delivery: Available in select metro areas for orders before 10 AM
   - Economy delivery: 7–10 business days at reduced rates

2. SHIPMENT TRACKING
   - All orders are assigned a tracking ID at dispatch.
   - Customers can query shipment status via voice or web portal.
   - Real-time updates are provided every 4 hours.
   - Push notifications are sent on status changes (pending → in_transit → delivered).
   - Tracking is available 24/7 through the AI voice agent.

3. MISSING PACKAGES
   - Report missing packages within 30 days of expected delivery.
   - An investigation is launched within 24 hours of a report.
   - Replacement or refund is issued within 5–7 business days.
   - Customers are notified at every step of the investigation.

4. DAMAGED GOODS
   - Document damage with photos within 48 hours of delivery.
   - File a claim through the customer portal or voice agent.
   - Compensation is processed within 7–10 business days.
   - Full replacement or refund is provided for confirmed damage.

5. REROUTING
   - Rerouting requests must be made before dispatch.
   - Post-dispatch rerouting may incur a fee up to $25.
   - Rerouting within the same city is free of charge.
   - International rerouting is not currently supported.

6. ESCALATION
   - Complex issues are escalated to a human agent automatically.
   - Agent response time is guaranteed within 2 business hours.
   - Escalated tickets are assigned priority status.
   - Customers receive a ticket ID for follow-up tracking.

7. CANCELLATION
   - Orders in "pending" status can be cancelled at no charge.
   - Orders in "in_transit" status may incur a cancellation fee of $15.
   - Delivered orders cannot be cancelled — use the return process instead.
   - Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within 3–5 business days.

8. RETURNS
   - Return requests must be initiated within 14 days of delivery.
   - Items must be in original packaging and undamaged condition.
   - A prepaid return label is provided for eligible returns.
   - Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned item.

9. WAREHOUSE OPERATIONS
   - We operate 20 distribution centres across the United States.
   - Average warehouse utilisation is monitored in real-time.
   - Orders are assigned to the nearest warehouse with available capacity.
   - Warehouse load exceeding 90% triggers overflow routing.

10. FLEET MANAGEMENT
    - Our fleet comprises 25 active vehicles with professional drivers.
    - Vehicles are tracked in real-time via GPS.
    - Vehicle maintenance is performed on a strict schedule.
    - Vehicles in "maintenance" status are temporarily removed from dispatch.

11. PRICING
    - Pricing is based on distance, package weight, and delivery speed.
    - Volume discounts are available for enterprise clients.
    - Fuel surcharges may apply during periods of high fuel costs.

12. DATA & PRIVACY
    - Customer data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
    - Voice recordings are processed locally and not stored permanently.
    - Customers can request data deletion under our privacy policy.
    - We comply with all applicable data protection regulations.
